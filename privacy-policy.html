Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

1. Who We Are

NVY Creations is a web design and development studio based in Medellín, Colombia. When this policy refers to "we," "us," or "our," it refers to NVY Creations as the data controller responsible for your personal data.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect personal data only through our contact form on nvycreations.com. The data collected includes:

  • Name (required)
  • Email address (required)
  • Service preference (optional)
  • Message content (required)

We do not use cookies, analytics tracking, or any third-party monitoring scripts. All fonts are self-hosted — no requests are made to Google or other third-party font services when you visit our site.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the personal data you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry about our services
  • Prepare project proposals when requested
  • Communicate about ongoing projects

We will never use your email for marketing, newsletters, or promotional content unless you separately and explicitly opt in.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on legitimate interest (responding to your direct inquiry) and your explicit consent provided via the contact form checkbox. You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us.

5. Third-Party Data Processing

Your form submission is processed by Web3Forms (web3forms.com), which acts as our data processor. Web3Forms receives your form data and delivers it to our email. We recommend reviewing Web3Forms' Privacy Policy for details on their data handling practices.

6. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and complete any resulting project, typically no longer than 24 months after last communication. After this period, your data is deleted from our records. Web3Forms retains data according to their own retention policy.

7. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (including GDPR and Colombian Ley 1581 de 2012), you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — correct any inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your personal data
  • Restriction — request limited processing of your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to the processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within 15 business days (Colombian law) or 30 days (GDPR).

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed outside of your country of residence, including by Web3Forms. We take reasonable steps to ensure your data is treated securely and in accordance with this privacy policy.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your personal data, including HTTPS encryption for all data transmission and Content Security Policy headers to prevent unauthorized code execution.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
